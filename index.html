About Us

The Entrepreneurship Cell is a student-run initiative cultivating entrepreneurial thinking and leadership. We bridge students, mentors, and incubators through workshops, competitions, field visits, and speaker sessions.


Tagline: Think • Innovate • Implement

Vision

Build an entrepreneurial culture across disciplines with access to resources, mentors, and opportunities.

Mission

Connect students to incubators and industry; nurture innovative ideas toward real-world commercialization.

Objectives

  • Awareness & skill-building
  • Hands-on events & challenges
  • Mentorship & networking
  • Confidence & leadership
